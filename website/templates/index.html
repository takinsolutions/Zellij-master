Data Integration

it empowers organizations to bring together disparate data from a multitude of sources into a unified, coherent, and semantically rich ecosystem. This process involves extracting structured and unstructured data from databases, files, web services, and other repositories, and then harmonizing and transforming it to adhere to a common data model and ontology. The platform should provide tools and connectors to facilitate this data ingestion and integration, allowing users to map relationships and semantics across datasets. Data integration within a semantic context goes beyond traditional ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) processes by preserving the meaning and context of data attributes, enabling more advanced querying, reasoning, and analytics. .
